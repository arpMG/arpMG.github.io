The Study

This study investigated the effect of someone's personality type on the genres of music they may prefer. In this below section, the study as a whole will be discussed.

Overall

This study investigated the possible effect of an individual's personality type on their taste in music. In short, could we say that everyone who is more introverted is more likely to enjoy metal and those extroverted are more likely to enjoy dance music?

As such, it was hypothesised that by categorising people into personality types, one could accurately and reliably pinpoint genres of music an individual will prefer.

Personality Types

But, the first hurdle in all this, how do we measure someone’s personality type? Looking into past research into this field, it is clear that the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (or MBTI for short) is reliable. More on this in the interview with a Psychologist currently working at Mentone Grammar School, Maddy Prowse, which is detailed in the Resources section.

Music Taste

Now, while we may be able to reliably pinpoint an individual’s personality type, it must brought into question how we will measure music taste. After sufficient thought it was decided that the best genres of music to test for would be: EDM, Rock, Metal, Rap, Classical, Blues, Jazz and Indie. While even I, as a music fanatic, felt that music genres were significantly more broad than this, for the sake of this study the eight genres was the best way to approach the situation. Once the survey had been created to determine individuals taste in music Gavin Cornish, an experienced composer and Direct of Music and Performing Arts at a south-eastern Grammar school was interviewed about the constructed survey. The full interview may be found in the resources section of this page. Through interview, my decision of how to narrow down music taste was validated, however, it was agreed that if more time, resources and experience were available more genres and those that are then would be in more depth to get a more solid idea of an individual's music tastes.

The Findings

After collecting data based on a wide variety of age groups (14-52 years) individuals personality type, music taste and basic demographic information was collected. With this anonymous data at my disposal, the data processing began.

Looking at the Surface

To begin the analysis of data, the surface information of the tested individuals was analysed. To start simply, individual's introversion/ extroversion was analysed, similarly to their gender to see if either of these may make one more likely to enjoy a particular music genre. Try clicking on the variable names to interact with the chart!

Based on the above chart, it can be seen that the research suggests that those who are introverted tend to enjoy Jazz, Metal and Rock more, whereas, Extroverted individuals tend to enjoy EDM, Rap and Indie music more so.

Meanwhile, on the note of gender the data would seem to suggest that males are more likely to enjoy metal, classical and jazz music, whereas, females are more likely to enjoy Indie, Blues and EDM music. Interestingly, the data suggests that both Rap and Rock appear to be just as likely for males and females to enjoy.

Personality Type To Music Taste

After all the analysis and manipulation of data was completed, it was discovered that certain personality types did seem to be more draw to certain music tastes. In the below Bar Chart you may select one of the four main personality types (Analyst, Diplomat, Sentinel or Explorer) to see how much they were found to enjoy a genre of music. That is, what percentage of individuals associated with that personality type indicated they enjoyed the genre.

Summary of the Types

  • It was hinted that Analysts most enjoyed genre was classical, with 80% indicating they enjoyed it.
  • Perhaps the most varied, It was suggested that Diplomats most enjoyed genres were blues, jazz and rock with 67% of these individuals indicating they enjoyed one or more of these genres. It is worth noting, however, that metal closely followed with 63%.
  • Explorers most enjoyed genre was suggested to be EDM, with 71% indicating that they enjoy it.
  • Sentinels most enjoy genre was EDM, sitting in at 83% indicating they enjoyed it.

In Conclusion

While there was a correlation found between personality type and most genre of music, due to the limited sample space which did not cover all personality types in practice it’s not possible to say the hypothesis is fully supported. Particularly beyond the first preference of music, there was no further correlation found.

From the prior analysis of secondary data it can be seen that past research did find particular personality types to be drawn to particular styles and genres of music. This ultimately aligns with the results of the analysed primary data.

Therefore, the hypothesis is supported to an extent by the data collected as it appears possible to estimate a genre of music an individual may most enjoy based solely upon their personality type.
