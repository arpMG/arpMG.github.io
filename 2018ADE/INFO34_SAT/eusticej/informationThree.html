Are they environmental?

When gathering data, it became apparent that electrical cars might not be as fuel efficient as first thought. Most of this data was secondary data, however it was interesting to see what the respondents thought of electrical cars being environmentaly freindly.


Are they friendly?

Most respondents said 'yes', however according to research, if all of Australia used electric cars, we would polute more then we do now. This is because our main way of getting electricity is burning coal, so Australia needs to find better energy alternatives before we use electrical cars.

Whats the most apparent energy?

Australia's energy is 73% coal generated meaning most of our energy needs to generated via polution. The remaining 27% of generaded energy is friendly and green with the most being gas and hydro powered. If we can change the coal generation to be less then 40% of our energy, then electrical cars will start to be a better alternative, but that may take many years to achieve.
