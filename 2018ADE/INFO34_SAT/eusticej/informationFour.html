What do we think?

Respondents were asked to provide their own opinions on electrical cars and why they arn't found on the roads very often. Results provide an interesting awnser that needs to be addressed in order for Australia to start using electrical cars.


What do we think of electrical cars?

The results show that most people like electrical cars, however everyone who didn't like or hate them said they were also not environmentaly friendly. There were 10 respondents who liked or said they were alright electrical cars and thought they were not environmentaly friendly aswell, so it can't be deemed the only deciding factor.

Why arn't these cars already on the road?

Its shown through this data that respondents think electrical cars arn't accessible to the public. Its interesting as Tesla and other company stores are open to sell these cars, but not everywhere and the initial expense is huge. Another interesting point is no one said they were not on he road due to not being environmentaly friendly.
