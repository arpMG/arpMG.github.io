Regularity Of Food Purchasing


As shown in the radar chart, 42% of those surveyed shop once a week indicating an organised approach to meal planning, potentially reducing waste from impulse buying and random purchases that do not form a structured meal plan.

The survey showed people rarely shop more than three times a week, with only 35% responding to this timeframe.

The chart clearly demonstrates with the clustering of responses in the one and three times a week area that this is how many times a week the majority of those surveyed shop.

Small and Large Shopping


The survey showed there is a clear correlation between the size of shops and the amount of rubbish that is being produced.

36% of people shopping buy small quantities of food and 87% of these people feel they have minimal food waste.

Whereas, the 64% of people that have large shops, 35% of them feel they have greater food wastage.
