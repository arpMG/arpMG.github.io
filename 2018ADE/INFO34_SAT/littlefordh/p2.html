Awareness Of How Plastic Bags Impact In The Environment


When asked the question:
"Did you know that it is estimated that 500 billion single use plastic bags are used each year in the world?"
63% of those surveyed acknowledge that they have no idea of the extent of plastic bags used.

However, when asked:
"Are you concerned about the impact of single use plastic bags on the environment?"
49% of those surveyed responded yes, and want to change to stop using single use plastic bags.

Regularity Of Customer Use

  • 69% of the people surveyed use 1 to 5 plastic bags a week.
  • 23% of those surveyed use 6 to 10 plastic bags a week
  • 8% use 11 to 15 plastic bags a week

Even though only 8% of people use 11 to 15 plastic bags a week this is still a large number of plastic bags entering and harming the environment.
