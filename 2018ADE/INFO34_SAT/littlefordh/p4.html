Issues Surrounding Landfill


The survey investigated the understanding the participants had regarding the impact of landfill on the environment.

Of those surveyed 82% were aware that landfill results in toxins leaching into the environment, greenhouse waste and damage to the water table but only 42% of people had considered or investigated the issues surrounding the location of landfill.

Motivators To Reduce Rubbish

The participants in the survey were asked to consider what would encourage them to reduce their rubbish.

Those surveyed responded were motivated by:

  1. Paying extra for bins 40%

  2. Discount on bin rates 38%

  3. Awareness of Issue 22%

What Makes Up Most Of Your Rubbish

The top items that are filling our rubbish bins are:

  1. Wrappers
  2. Food Waste
  3. Garden Waste
  4. Other

The items listed above are making up our landfill, that is getting out of hand with the quantity of rubbish going into it.
