The Issues

Daily Food Waste

Awareness
  • Australia produces enough food to feed 60 million people each year.
    Australian households and businesses waste 4 million tonnes of food annually, this could fill the Melbourne Cricket Ground six times over!

  • Major supermarkets such as Woolworths and Coles contribute to waste as they refuse to sell fruits and vegetables that don't meet their specifications.

  • Environmentally, food waste accounts for about 8% of all global greenhouse gas emissions.
